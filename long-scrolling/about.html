Everybody Dreams

My long scrolling website consist of different images that the character in the story is dreaming about. The images and background are drawn by me. The context for this website is that our dreams meaning our main goals in life sometimes can become reality if we try hard enough. I used the characters dreams as a metaphor for the common goals most of us want to reach. Sometimes we have nightmares like life crisis, but we eventually wake up from it and everything is ok. This website shows that reaching our goals is a process and sometimes we stubble upon troubles that slows us down but we eventually wake up from our nightmares.
